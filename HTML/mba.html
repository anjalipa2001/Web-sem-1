MASTERS OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION


Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering is affiliated to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technological University and M.G. University. The applications for M.B.A. course at the college are now open. It is a 2 years programme with Academic Sessions and Training and Placement Activities. The total intake of the course is 120 seats.Application for the course of MBA which is approved by AICTE. It offers specializationsin Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, Systems, International Business and Production and Operations Management.The basic eligibility to apply for this course is successfully completed Bachelors Degree. Admission is based on the Merit list published after evaluating an applicant’s performance at Bachelor level, MAT/CMAT or equivalent exam, Group Discussion and Interviews.
