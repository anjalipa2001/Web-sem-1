MASTERS OF COMPUTER APPLICATION


The Department of Computer Applications, SNGCE was established in the year 2009 to impart the best available education in post graduate course of Computer Applications. It is now recognized as one of the leading departments affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University, Kerala. The state- of – the art infrastructure and amenities to match the very best in the state makes it unique among aristocrats. It is committed to impart quality education in the fields of Computer Science, Information Technology and their applications in academics, industries and enterprises which are growing in leaps and bounds. Since its inception, the department has proved itself to be outstanding in University Results, Rankings and placements. The curriculum is so made that the course provides a good theoretical foundation through high quality teaching complemented by extensive practical training.
