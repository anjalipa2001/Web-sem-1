BTech


SNGCE offers four-year B.Tech degree in 6 specializations with a total intake of 570 students at the UG level. The college is known to offer Naval Architecture & Ship Engineering specialization in B.Tech. All admissions to the course are based on scores/ranks of KEAM conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations, Kerala or KEE. The final merit list is prepared by the college after considering the marks obtained in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry or equivalent subjects in the qualifying examination and marks obtained in the same subjects in any of the two state entrance exams. The marks obtained in the qualifying exam are subjected to normalization process.
